Liam Byrne hails from the United States, with possible connections to Baltimore, Maryland. His previous occupations include being a firefighter for the BFD and then a military soldier for a Special Forces squad known as the Ragged Ravens, serving in both professions alongside a close friend which he has known since childhood. At some point, he became affiliated with Kingdom Corporation, eventually becoming a veteran of their subsidiary K/SEC.[1][2] After the events of First Light, the secretive VALORANT Protocol was founded, with Byrne being the first agent to join them and eventually becoming its leader and commander, "Brimstone".[3] Around 11 AFL, Brimstone began overseeing efforts from his office within the Range on replicating the Alpha-Omega teleportals used in the Everett-Linde Research Facility that allowed for travel between Earth-1 and Mirror Earth. Brimstone, alongside Viper, then personally went on an operation which led to an encounter with French weapons designer Vincent Fabron. The encounter did not go smoothly, but nevertheless saw Brimstone welcoming Fabron as the Protocol's 18th agent in order to make progress with the teleportal.
